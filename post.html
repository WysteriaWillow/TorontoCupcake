The Secret to the Perfect Swirl

Head Baker December 2025

Getting that professional bakery look at home is one of the most requested tips from our community.

To get that iconic Toronto Cupcake look, you need three essential things: cold frosting, a large star tip, and steady pressure. Many beginners make the mistake of using room-temperature frosting, which causes the swirl to sag under its own weight before it even reaches the table.

Start your swirl at the very edge of the cupcake and move in a clockwise circle, slowly building a pyramid toward the center. Ensure you are overlapping the previous layer slightly to create height. Don't stop the pressure until you lift the bag quickly at the very top!
