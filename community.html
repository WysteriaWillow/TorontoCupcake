Our Local Heart

Since we opened our doors, Toronto Cupcake has been more than just a bakery. We believe in supporting our neighbors, which is why 10% of our seasonal cupcake sales go back to local Toronto youth programs. Our mission is to create a ripple effect of kindness, one sprinkle at a time.
